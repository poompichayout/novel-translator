In these seven days, Du Xingchuan was at his wits' end. He could only control all his forces along the river and struggle. During these seven days, Lin Xun had been practicing at the bottom of the river. His injury was indeed very serious. He was bombarded by five Ziying Battleships all the way. Although they didn't hit his vital parts, the impact force of the horrible aftermath was not so easy to bear. In the cave, Lin Xun sat cross-legged like a sculpture. Since advancing to the Earth-gang Realm, all the spiritual power in his body had transformed into Earth-gang power. This was not Lin Xun's own aura, but the Amrita Saint Pearl in the sea of his consciousness! Under this rhythm fluctuation, Lin Xun clearly perceived that there seemed to be wisps of mysterious "water essence" in the water in the river being drawn into his body and then integrated into the Amrita Saint Pearl in the sea of his consciousness. This scene surprised Lin Xun and he finally realized that he had been mistaken about one thing all the time. If the Amrita Saint Pearl wanted to wake up from silence, it naturally needed to replenish its strength. It couldn't rely on self-recovery! And the strength it needed was in the water! When Lin Xun realized this, he found that when he practiced in the next few days, the Amrita Saint Pearl in the sea of his consciousness was also practicing in a unique way. However, what it absorbed was the strands of profound 'water element energy' contained within the water! What surprised Lin most was that although the "water power" was eventually absorbed by the Amrita Saint Pearl, it imperceptibly moistened and washed every inch of his skin, flesh, tendons and bones, viscera, meridians and acupoints when it passed through his body. As a result, the serious injuries Lin Xun had suffered were quickly healed. The wounds that originally needed ten to fifteen days to completely heal were now completely healed in just seven days! Moreover, compared to the past, his physique was much stronger after he had completely recovered! All of this was naturally because of the mysterious' energy of water. ' The reason why Lin Xun didn't leave was that he wanted to take this opportunity to make Amrita Saint Pearl recover from silence. But until today, the wonderful rhythm released by the Amrita Saint Pearl disappeared and fell into silence again. Lin Xun couldn't help being stunned. After a while, he thought thoughtfully that the 'water element energy' in the river was too common to meet the needs of the Amrita Saint Pearl. Well, it wouldn't be too late to find a chance to explore the mystery of the Amrita Saint Pearl in the future. While thinking, Lin Xun opened his eyes from meditation, and a frightening luster flashed in his deep and clear black eyes.
